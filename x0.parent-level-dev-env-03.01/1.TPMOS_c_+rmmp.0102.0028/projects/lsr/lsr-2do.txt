## OVERVIEW
This project is a TPMOS-compliant refactor and expansion of the "Martian Street Race" (WSR) simulation. The goal is to migrate the economy to the Lunar surface and implement a fully autonomous, bot-managed financial system.

---

## 🟥 PHASE 1: THE METABOLISM (IMMEDIATE)
The "Missing Heart" of WSR must be implemented as standalone TPM Ops.
- [ ] **Payroll Op:** Create `lsr/ops/payroll_op.c`. Should read a Piece's `state.txt`, deduct wages, and log to `master_ledger.txt`.
- [ ] **Tax Op:** Create `lsr/ops/tax_op.c`. Should calculate taxes based on corporate revenue and transfer to Government Pieces.
- [ ] **Dividend Op:** Create `lsr/ops/dividend_op.c`. Should distribute profit percentages to shareholders.
