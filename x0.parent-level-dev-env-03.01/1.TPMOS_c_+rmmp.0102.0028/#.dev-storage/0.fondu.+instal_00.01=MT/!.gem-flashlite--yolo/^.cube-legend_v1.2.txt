Cube Face Legend - Coordinate System and Colors
===============================================

This document defines the coordinate system and face colors for the Legend Block cube visualization.

COORDINATE SYSTEM:
- X-axis (red) : Left/Right (-x = left, +x = right)
- Y-axis (green): Up/Down (-y = down, +y = up)  
- Z-axis (blue): Forward/Back (-z = back, +z = forward)

____________________________________________
green = (y+)faces upward along positive Y-axis(top)
brown = (y-)faces downward along negative Y-axis (bottom)
back = (z+) faces backwards along positive z-axis(back)
eyes = (z-)faces forward along negative z-axis(front)

right = x+faces right along positive X-axis (right-hand)
left = x- faces left along negative X-axis(left-hand)

-----------------------------

green = (y+)faces upward along positive Y-axis(top)
brown = (y-)faces downward along negative Y-axis (bottom)
back = (z+) faces backwards along positive z-axis(back) = black
eyes = (z-)faces forward along negative z-axis(front) =  white

right = x+faces right along positive X-axis (right-hand)= red
left = x- faces left along negative X-axis(left-hand) = blue 

_____________________________
REFERENCE:
The coordinate system used is API-based: [z, y, x] where z is the layer,
y is the row, and x is the column, but internally stored as [x, y, z].
